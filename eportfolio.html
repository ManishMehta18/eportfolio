1. What is Full Stack Development?

Full Stack Explained

Full stack development refers to the development of both front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) portions of a web application. A full stack developer works with:

  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, frameworks like React/Angular
  • Back-end: Node.js, Python (Django/Flask), PHP, Java, databases
  • DevOps: Deployment, CI/CD, cloud services

This portfolio demonstrates my front-end skills across all key technologies.
